Interactive Anatomy

🧠 Brain

The brain is the command center of your nervous system. It processes pain signals, regulates dopamine levels, and controls neuroplasticity—your brain's ability to rewire itself.

❤️ Heart

Your heart is intricately connected to your nervous system. Heart rate variability reflects nervous system health and is key to managing stress and pain.

🫁 Lungs

Breathing directly impacts nervous system regulation. Deep breathing activates the parasympathetic nervous system, reducing pain and promoting relaxation.

🟨 Liver

Your liver processes neurotransmitters and toxins, impacting dopamine metabolism and overall nervous system function.

🟣 Stomach

The gut-brain axis shows that your digestive system and brain communicate extensively. Gut health directly affects neurotransmitter production and mood.

🧴 Spine

The spine houses the spinal cord, your primary pain highway. Understanding spinal health is crucial for managing chronic pain and neuroplasticity.
